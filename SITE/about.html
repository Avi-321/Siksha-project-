What We Do

  • Shiksha works for day to day education for the children from underprivileged sections of the society
  • We help those children for preparing various competitive exams like JEE, NTSE and Navodya.
  • We maintain the balance between studies and development by organizing cultural workshops and fests.
  • For regular improvement of their performance we take monthly tests.
  • We organize technical workshops for supplementing theory knowledge with practical.
  • We organize educational tours to museum and planetoriums.
  • We organize cultural workshops for their overall development.

Mission

  • To Aim to teach students who can afford education.
  • To provide educational materials such as books, notebooks, class room and so on for poor children.
  • To provode awareness about better career, fields, current world's knowledge etc to children.
  • To launch awareness programs for encouragement of people to educate their children.
  • To Nurture and bring out the talent in various extra curricular activities such sports, dancing, arts, singing etc.
  • To teach proper health and sanitary practices for the welfare of themselves and surroundings.
  • To support personal development social responsibility, and a sense of self-worth.

Achievements So Far

  • A rural area student from 2015- 16 batch cleared JEE Mains with Final Rank: 60,200 (OBC: 15,000)
  • Viraj, a student of class 12th achieved position of district topper class 12th.
  • Sanjana, a student of class 10th achieved the position of school topper.
